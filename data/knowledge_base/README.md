# Knowledge base

Source documents for retrieval-augmented generation:

- research notes and memos
- earnings call transcripts
- regulatory filings (10-K, 10-Q, 8-K)
- internal investment policy documents

Documents in this folder are ingested by the `alphalens.rag` pipeline,
chunked, embedded, and indexed in Qdrant. Do not commit confidential or
copyrighted material.
